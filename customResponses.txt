Usage Guidelines
Frequency Recommendations:
High frequency (50-60%): Categories 1-2 (Active Listening & Neutral)
Medium frequency (25-30%): Categories 3-4 (Validating & Positive)
Low frequency (10-15%): Categories 5-7 (Excited, Pauses, Encouraging)
Minimal frequency (5%): Categories 8-9 (Reflective & Empathetic Sounds)
Context-Based Selection:
User is venting/frustrated: Use Categories 1, 3, 6
User is sharing good news: Use Categories 4, 5
User is thinking/processing: Use Categories 1, 6, 8
User is telling a story: Use Categories 1, 2, 7
User sounds sad/down: Use Categories 1, 3, 6
Timing:
Deliver acknowledgments every 5-10 seconds of user speech
Vary the phrases to avoid repetition
Match the energy level of the user (calm vs. excited)
Keep responses under 3 words when possible
Voice Characteristics:
Tone: Warm, gentle, non-judgmental
Pace: Slightly slower than the user's pace
Volume: Moderate, not overpowering
Pitch: Natural, conversational range
